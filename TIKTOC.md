# Principles of Management: with LLMs
## Compact Tik TOC Architecture

**Book type:** MBA / management textbook  
**Status:** Retrospective architecture for developed content  

## Core Thesis

This book teaches management as the practice of coordinating people, goals, decisions, systems, culture, and change, with LLMs used to support reflection, communication, and analysis.

## Primary Reader

MBA students, managers, team leads, founders, and professionals entering leadership roles.

## Learning Arc

1. **Managing self and teams:** roles, motivation, communication, decision-making, and conflict.
2. **Managing systems:** structure, strategy, operations, culture, and performance.
3. **Managing change:** innovation, ethics, AI, crisis, and organizational learning.

## Chapter Architecture

Each chapter should begin with a managerial situation, diagnose the organizational dynamic, and produce a practical management artifact.

## Terminal Capability

The reader can analyze management problems, choose appropriate interventions, and communicate decisions with awareness of human and organizational consequences.

## Adoption Risks

- Management content can become generic without cases.
- AI-generated advice can flatten context.
- Ethical and power dynamics must remain explicit.
